Prerequisites for Programming Mentors in VEX

If you're interested in volunteering as a programming mentor for the VEX Robotics Team, here are some prerequisites and skills you should have:

  • Experience in programming languages like Python, C++, and block-based coding.
  • Understanding of robotics principles and concepts, such as sensors, actuators, and control systems.
  • Ability to communicate complex concepts to students in a clear and concise manner.
  • Patience and enthusiasm for teaching and mentoring students of different ages and skill levels.
  • Familiarity with the VEX Robotics platform, VEXcode, and competition rules is a plus.

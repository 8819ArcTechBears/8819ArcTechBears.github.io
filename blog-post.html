Get Involved in VEX Robotics Team

Parents

Parents can play a crucial role in supporting their children's participation in the VEX Robotics Team. Here's how you can help:

  • Encourage and support your child's interest in robotics and STEM education.
  • Assist the team with organizing events, fundraisers, and meetings.
  • Help with transportation to and from competitions, practices, and other events.
  • Share your expertise and skills with the team, such as mentoring, coaching, or providing technical assistance.

Volunteers/ Mentors

Volunteers are essential to the success of the VEX Robotics Team. There are many ways you can contribute:

  • Mentor students in programming, design, and engineering.
  • Assist with event planning, logistics, and organization.
  • Offer technical expertise in areas like electronics, mechanics, and software development.
  • Help promote the team and its activities within the community and online.

Learn Block, Python, and C++ Coding with VEXcode EXP

Experience the power of coding with VEXcode EXP, a versatile coding platform that supports Block, Python, and C++ programming languages. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced programmer, VEXcode EXP has features that cater to all skill levels.

VEXcode EXP is available on a wide range of devices, including Windows PCs, Macs, Chromebooks, iPads*, and Android tablets*.

Discover the Benefits of VEX EXP Coding

With VEXcode EXP, you can:

  • Learn programming concepts in a fun and engaging way.
  • Develop problem-solving and critical thinking skills.
  • Easily transition between Block, Python, and C++ programming languages.
  • Access a wealth of resources, tutorials, and sample projects to support your learning journey.

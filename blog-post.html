Get Involved in VEX Robotics Team

Parents

Parents can play a crucial role in supporting their children's participation in the VEX Robotics Team. Here's how you can help:

    Encourage and support your child's interest in robotics and STEM education. Assist the team with organizing events, fundraisers, and meetings. Help with transportation to and from competitions, practices, and other events. Share your expertise and skills with the team, such as mentoring, coaching, or providing technical assistance.

Volunteers/ Mentors

Volunteers are essential to the success of the VEX Robotics Team. There are many ways you can contribute:

  • Mentor students in programming, design, and engineering.
  • Assist with event planning, logistics, and organization.
  • Offer technical expertise in areas like electronics, mechanics, and software development.
  • Help promote the team and its activities within the community and online.

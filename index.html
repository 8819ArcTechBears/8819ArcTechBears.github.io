Austin Achieve is a College Ready, K-12 school which gives underprivileged students educational opportunities not typically accessible to them. Our team is funded entirely by grants and parent sponsors

Outreach

Our first year in 2022 We visited several underclassmen advisory classes to present the FRC Core Values, Kahoot game to expose them to Robotics, our team, and how they could support Our most impactful outreach was putting together/hosting our high school’s first in person science fair. We were able to showcase over 140 STEM projects giving those scholars an opportunity to showcase their passion for science to our school and community. This was many of the scholars first time doing a science fair project!

Female Lead

Our team is female Led. A majority of our members identify as women, which is rare. Most teams are usually majority male.

What is FIRST Robotics Competition?

News
2022 FIRST in Texas District

Combining the excitement of sport with the rigors of science and technology. We call FIRST Robotics Competition the ultimate Sport for the Mind. High-school student participants call it “the hardest fun you’ll ever have.” Under strict rules, limited time and resources, teams of students are challenged to raise funds, design a team "brand," hone teamwork skills, and build and program industrial-size robots to play a difficult field game against like-minded competitors. It’s as close to real-world engineering as a student can get. Volunteer professional mentors lend their time and talents to guide each team. Each season ends with an exciting FIRST Championship.

Our Supporters and Leadership Team

Our team's mission is to provide underprivileged, minority, and female students with a space to explore the world of STEM. Through the design of our club, students can focus on their specific interests and collaborate with mentors and teams to ignite new passions. We strive to prepare our students to succeed in professional STEM environments beyond high school. We are the ARC-TECH BEARS! - FRC Team 8819.

Read our story

Our founding

ARC-TECH BEARS! - FRC Team 8819 is grateful for the generous support of our founding sponsor, Dell Technologies. With their contribution, we can continue our mission to provide underprivileged, girls, and minority youth with opportunities to explore the world of STEM. We have been able to purchase new equipment, participate in competitions, and further our outreach efforts in the community, all thanks to Dell | Latino Connection volunteers and members. We thank them for their commitment to our team and look forward to our continued partnership.

Growth & beyond

While we are grateful for our current sponsor and their generous contribution, we acknowledge that FRC (FIRST Robotics Competition) is an expensive program to maintain. The cost of travel, after-school snacks, and equipment can add up quickly, and as we continue to grow and expand our team, we will need additional financial support to sustain our efforts. We hope to find more sponsors and supporters who share our vision and mission, and who are willing to invest in the future of underprivileged, minority youth in STEM fields. With your support, we can continue to offer a safe and supportive space for scholars in JV/VARSITY team to explore their passions and develop the skills they need to succeed in the professional world beyond high school.
